Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Convert: Currency Converter ("Convert") respects your privacy.

Convert does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not require

  • Account registration
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location access
  • Contacts access

Exchange rates

Currency exchange rates are downloaded from public third-party providers over the internet. These requests do not include personal identifiers.

Local storage

Selected currencies, preferences, conversion history, and cached exchange rates are stored locally on your device only.

Analytics & advertising

Convert does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies. No user data is sold or shared with third parties.

In-app purchases

Optional tips are processed by Apple. Convert does not receive or store your payment information.

Widget

The Home Screen widget reads shared preferences and cached rates from an App Group container on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.
